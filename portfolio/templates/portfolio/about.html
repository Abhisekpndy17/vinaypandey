Introduction

The firm “CS Vinay Pandey & Associates” Company Secretaries was set up in the year 2019 by CS Vinay Pandey who has more than four years of experience in the profession. CS Vinay Pandey is a qualified Company Secretary and Associate member of Institute of Company Secretaries of India. He is also a Law Graduate (LLB) from VBSPU, Jaunpur.

He is a full-time practicing Company Secretary based in Delhi and rendering services on PAN India basis. Earlier, he was working with a Corporate for last 4 years. He has completed his internship from Ansal Housing & Construction Ltd. (Real Estate, NSE & BSE Listed Company) and has over four years of experience working in Corporates.

He is also co-founder of TP Films LLP (a film producing Company). He is also visiting faculty of Professional Study Point and providing strategic study planning to CS Professional and CS Executive programmes students for the following subjects: Financial and Strategic Management (CS Executive New Syllabus) Company Law (CS Executive & Professional) Tax Laws (CS Executive & Professional) Corporate Restructuring, Insolvency, Liquidation & Winding-up (CS Professional New Syllabus) Financial, Treasury & Forex Management (CS Professional Old Syllabus)
